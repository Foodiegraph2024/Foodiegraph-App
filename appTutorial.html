Foodiegraph App Tutorial (Original Proposal)

  • Step 1
    Scan
    Scan the QR code below to visit the Foodiegraph webpage. 掃描 QR code 前往 Foodiegraph (AI 視界食典) 網頁。
  • Step 2
    Take a Photo
    Turn on your camera and take a photo of any ingredient of your choice. 開啟相機並拍下任意所選的食材。
  • Step 3
    Upload
    Upload the photo of the ingredient you’ve taken to the homepage of Foodiegraph. 在 Foodiegraph (AI 視界食典) 首頁中上傳所拍攝的食材照片。
  • Step 4
    Generate!
    Click “generate” and wait a few seconds for the app to identify the photographed ingredient and generate recipe recommendations. 按 “generate (產生)” 並等待應用程式辨識圖片中的食材以及自動產生食譜推薦。
  • Step 5
    Enjoy!
    Your recipe is automatically generated! Browse the recipe with step-by-step instructions and get ready to cook a delicious meal for yourself! 您的 食譜已自動產生！瀏覽帶有詳細步驟說明的食譜，並準備好為自己烹製一頓美味佳餚吧！
