Our Mission/Vision

We believe good things come to those who don’t waste.

We believe in ethical consumption and sustainable shopping habits.

We believe health and wellness comes with nutritious meals and a healthy diet.

We believe in being transparent and never putting our bottom line before what’s best for the planet.

We’re Foodiegraph, and we’re glad you’re here.

In recent years, the streets of Taiwan have been swarming with food delivery motorcycles that overwhelm the streets with increased chaos. But the challenges posed by the food delivery industry didn't stop there—the amount of food and plastic waste substantially increased with the rise of the industry. Food delivery involves a copious amount of one-use containers and utensils that might ultimately harm the environment or contribute more to the carbon footprint if burnt; the industry relies heavily on natural resources that contemporary policies are trying to preserve, making it a complete antithesis to government sustainability plans.

We aim to reduce the food waste and plastic waste that the food delivery industry brought by making cooking simple, accessible, and sustainable. From AI-generated recipe recommendations based on photographed ingredients, to tutorials, to even the friend feature to share recipes and culinary tips, we are the one stop shop for cooks of all levels and those who hope to contribute to sustainable initiatives.

We are inspired by these factors…

Traffic

Taiwan is notorious for the traffic; the delivery drivers on mopeds are not helping.

Quality

Delivered food might be cold and/or not fresh.

Unheathy Cuisine

Fast foods increase the amount of unhealthy food consumption.

Loneliness

Rates for depression and loneliness are rising. Going outdoors to buy ingredients could potentially lower this rate (even doing groceries count).

Skills

We noticed not a lot of our peers possess culinary skills, and many have the motivation to learn cooking but are lacking resources and support.

Sustainability

By not using as many one-time containers, the reduction of wastes can be achieved.
